# SOURCES — pedagogy citation registry (`[Pn]` namespace)

Stable, append-only citation keys for the pedagogy library. **Cross-course** namespace, distinct
from any per-course `SOURCES.md` (`[Sn]`) — see `TAXONOMY.md`. Once assigned, a key is never
reused for a different source.

**Provenance**: primary source material was an external deep-research pass
(`perplexity_output.md`, 2026-07-10). During curation every ambiguous citation was re-verified
against Crossref / ERIC / NCBI; corrections to the research pass's attributions are noted per
entry. The research output's unreferenced footnote span (`[^1_48]`–`[^1_73]`) was **not**
imported. Reliability judgment here is **tracing** (the claim maps to this source), not
re-verification of each source's own findings.

**Marks**:

- *(indirect)* — known only via secondary summaries; no direct full text or DOI consulted.
- *(secondary link)* — the research pass linked a third-party summary/mirror; the canonical
  reference is given here instead.
- *(popular source)* — origin of a myth's claim; cited for provenance, **never** as evidence.
- *(supporting)* — weaker venue or study type; use to corroborate, not to anchor a claim.
